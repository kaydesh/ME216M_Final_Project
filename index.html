Elevator Pitch

The process of gymming, to a large extent, is an individual activity today. Social connections are powerful. We do stuff that we would not have done, had it not been for people around us. Our product Forza brings a new level of social connection to the gym. We aim to leverage the power of social connections to enhance individual fitness.

User Interactions

When you are going to the gym, Forza remains in defualt state. The user can go to the 'Work Out' mode by pressing the start button. The user can go to the 'Pause Work Out' mode by pressing the pause button. The user can check the stats by pressing the stats button. The device can track of the lift out, time spent working out, day's score.

Social Interactions

Forza values the bonds you share with your friends. It creates an interface where people who matter to you can interact and tell their stories. It also keeps track of how you have been doing over a period of time.
